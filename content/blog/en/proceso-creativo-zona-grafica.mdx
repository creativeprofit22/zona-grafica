## We don't start at the computer

Our creative process has been refined over more than 30 years, and the first lesson we learned is that good design never starts on screen. It starts at a table, with a coffee (probably from Cardo Café) and an honest conversation.

Where does your project come from? What do you want to communicate? Who is your audience? These questions seem simple, but the answers define everything that comes next. We've learned that one hour of good conversation saves weeks of revisions.

## Research before creation

San Miguel de Allende is a place where many cultures and aesthetics converge. That's why, before we design, we research: your market, your competition, trends in your industry, and also the cultural context where your brand will live.

From that research, what we call the visual compass is born: a document that defines color palette, typography, photography style, and communication tone. This document isn't decorative. It's the foundation on which we build everything.

## Sketches, not final files

Young designers want to reach the final file from day one. We've learned that the best results come from exploring freely first. We sketch quickly, try different directions, and present real options — not minimal variations of the same concept.

When we design posters for the Cervantino Festival or the annual image for GIFF, that exploration process is where the magic happens. Sometimes the best idea comes from the third sketch. Sometimes from the tenth.

## Refinement through feedback

Design doesn't happen in isolation. We present, listen, and refine. But we don't believe in design by committee. Our experience allows us to guide the process and stand behind the decisions that work.

The final result isn't just attractive. It's functional, scalable, and designed to live across every format your brand needs — from a business card to a 26-foot billboard.

## The relationship doesn't end at delivery

We deliver files, brand manuals, and the tools so you can apply your identity with confidence. But more importantly, we stay available. Many of our clients have been with us for years. GIFF for 22 editions, for example.

We believe in long relationships, not transactions. Your success is our best portfolio.
